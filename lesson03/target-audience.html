Target Audience

The target audience for this website is someone who is in Southern Idaho who wants to know what the weather will be like.

Personas

Persona 1

  • Fictional Name: Benjamin Young
  • Job Title: Security Guard
  • Demographics:
    • 36 years old
    • Married with 2 kids
    • Veteran
  • Goals: To do a good day's work at his outdoor security guard job and to be prepared for any situation.
  • Enviornment: He is tech-compitent but doesn't want to be overwhelmed with details. His job requires him to frequently go outside no matter the weather.

Persona 2

  • Fictional Name: Lindsay Kennedy
  • Job Title: College Student
  • Demographics:
    • 19 years old
    • Single
    • Chemestry Major
  • Goals: To get good grades and save up money to buy a car.
  • Enviornment: She does not own a car and has to walk for 15 minutes each way to get to campus.

Scenarios

  3. Will soccer practice be cancelled?
  4. When will it snow?
